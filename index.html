Demographics

Demography of Rosario

Rosario is one of the densest municipalities in Cavite — home to the pioneering Cavite Economic Zone that drove the province's industrial revolution.

In-migration has been a continuous phenomenon since the early 1990s, with people drawn by employment opportunities in the economic zone. The municipality covers 768.36 hectares with a present population density of 147 per hectare. The potential labor force (15 years and above) comprises 72.67% of the total population, mostly engaged in fishing and trade, with a growth rate of 3.53%.
